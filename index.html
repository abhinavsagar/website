Hi! I just completed my undergrad at VIT Vellore. My research areas are bayesian deep learning, generative models, variational inference etc on the theoretical side and medical imaging, autonomous driving etc on the application side. More broadly, I am interested in deep learning and computer vision with a bayesian approach taking uncertainty into account. The communities I follow are NeurIPS, ICLR, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, MICCAI, MIDL, WACV and UAI.

I spent the summer of 2019 in Tessact where I worked on product recommendation using variational autoencoders. I spent the summer of 2018 in Tata Group where I trained a neural network on power grid electricity consumption data to predict the load 24 hours ahead of the actual generation.

Besides research, I enjoy travelling, playing guitar and cooking (also eating).
